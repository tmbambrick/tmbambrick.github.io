Ingredients

  • pasta
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbslp butter
  • cheese packet

Directions

  1. Boil Water on Stove
  2. Pour in pasta and cook for 8 mins
  3. Drain pasta and place back into pot
  4. Mix in milk, butter, and cheese
  5. Pour into bowl and enjoy!
