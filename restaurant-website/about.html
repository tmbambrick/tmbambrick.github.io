Our Founder


Michael


Michael has studied throughout Asia and is passionate about bringing Asian Cuisine from Japan, Vietnam, and Thailand. Michael wants to establish this restaurant on Long Island to introduce the area to the Asian dishes that he is passionate about. He has worked with collaborators to make sure the menu is authentic.

Ezo wants to bring authentic Asian cuisine to Long Island. Our award-winning chefs want to bring only the best ingredients to your table. Ezo has been providing high-quality dishes since our opening in 2015.
